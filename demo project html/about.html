About E-Voting

Electronic voting (also known as e-voting) is voting that uses electronic means to either aid or take care
of casting and counting ballots.
Depending on the particular implementation, e-voting may use standalone electronic voting machines (also called EVM)
or computers connected to the Internet (online voting)

Electronic voting technology intends to speed the counting of ballots, reduce the cost of paying staff to count
votes manually and can provide improved accessibility for disabled voters
